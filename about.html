About Us!

The Founding

This post is written by Prabz

Prabz - London Tours was founded in 1995 by Chairman Prabz Patel. We successfully have been a dedicated tour service for foreigners and even locals from time to time for 21 years and we continue to bring great service. We offer great deals and prices for our tours and everything within the tour is absolutely free! The idea of a tour service first came to the Chairman from a lifelong dream of touring the world himself.

Opportunities to expand?

This post is written by Prabz

As a tour service in the outskirts of the City Of London, many opportunities have risen to expand our company and persuade more people to tour with us. We have officially set up our tour service in six different countries including the USA, Russia, Africa, Mexico and more. We hope that we continue to grow and bring more opportunities to set up our tour services in other places of the world.

Why choose us?

We have been known to provide the best deals with our tours in the London area. We are one of the best known tour companies that bring you the best of what London has to offer. We don't charge a lot but we give a lot in terms of great service and dedication to show you what London has to offer.

How can you benefit?

We offer festive deals and promotional offers so that everyone can benfit with tours to fit all occasions, such as a specially selected tour for a birthdy gift, tour deals set during certain school breaks for the kids such as Christmas tours, Easter tours and even for those celebrating Diwali and other celebrations.
